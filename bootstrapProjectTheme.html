Prime Minister

Narendra Damodardas Modi is an Indian politician who is the 16th and current Prime Minister of India, in office since May 2014. He was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014, and is the Member of Parliament for Varanasi.

President

Ram Nath Kovind (born 1 October 1945) is the 14th and current President of India, in office since 25 July 2017.[1] Previously he had served as the Governor of Bihar from 2015 to 2017[2][3] and was a Member of Parliament.

Defence Minister
